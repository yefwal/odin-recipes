Corn on the Cob

Description

I love it because it’s SO easy, and it yields the sweetest, juiciest corn on the cob you can make. The recipe is really straightforward, so I don’t have any big tips or tricks, but I will say one thing: the fresher your corn is, the better your corn on the cob will be. Enjoy!

How to Make Your Own Corn on the Cob

Ingredients

  • corn
  • butter
  • salt
  • pepper

Steps

  1. Remove the husks from the corn
  2. Cook the corn (3-5 minutes)
  3. Lather it up with the butter, salt, and pepper
